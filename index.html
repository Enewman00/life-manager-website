Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 5, 2026

Overview

Choreganized ("the app") is designed to help you track maintenance reminders, health appointments, car service, home upkeep, and personal tasks. Your privacy is important to us.

Data Storage

All personal data you enter into Choreganized — including reminders, schedules, entity names, and task details — is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that collect or store your personal information. No cloud sync is performed.

Purchase Information

Choreganized uses RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions. When you make a purchase, RevenueCat processes the transaction and stores a record of your purchase history and a pseudonymous user identifier. This data is used solely to validate your subscription status and restore purchases across devices. RevenueCat's privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Third-Party Services

The app does not include any analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or tracking pixels. The only third-party service integrated is RevenueCat for purchase management as described above.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, location, contacts, photos, health data, or any other personal information. We do not track your usage patterns or share data with advertisers.

Children's Privacy

Choreganized does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation and stores no personal data on external servers.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.
